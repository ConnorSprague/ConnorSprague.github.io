Aimless Pretention-89.7 FM KMNR

KMNR is a free format college radio station located on the campus of Missouri University of Science and Technology. Founded in 1961 and taking the FCC handle KMNR in 1971 KMNR is a completely student run college radio station focusing on local and college issues, broadcasting music in free format. As part of KMNR I had the unique opportunity to co-host a talk radio show called Aimless Pretention. The show focused on current events and issues with a satirical twist. The aim of the show was to educate and entertain listeners with dialogue focused around current events. From politics to social justice, no topic was off bounds. Please check out KMNR.ORG to learn more about the organization as well as listen live to the web broadcast.
