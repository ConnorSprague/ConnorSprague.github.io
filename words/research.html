Healthcare Gamification Research

Under the supervision of Dr. Elizabeth Cudney of the Engineering Management Department of Missouri University of Science and Technology I coauthored this paper. This paper was presented at the 6th International Conference on Applied Human Factors and Ergonomics. The paper details an ongoing research project involving the United States Department of Veterans Affairs medical system. The project aims to observe and improve the VA healthcare system utilizing industrial methodologies.

Social Media Gamification Research

In conjunction with the Healthcare Gamification research I participated in the Missouri University of Science and Technology's Undergraduate Research Conference. I presented a research project investigating the use of gamification techniques in social media enviornments, namely Reddit.com. I was awarded 1st place for my research and presentation.

Smart Power Management

Under the supervision of Dr. Sun Zeyi I am currently working on a journal paper focused on smart power management systems. This paper is to be an extension of the paper shown. The paper focuses on the utilization of heuristic methods to model and impact peak power consumption in a manufacturing enviornment.
