Missouri S&T Human Powered Vehicle

Human Powered Vehicle is an ASME sponsored college student design competition focues on the design and construction of high efficiency human powered vehicles.

I have participated on the Missouri S&T Human Powered Vehicle team since December of 2013.

I took on the position of Lead Fairing Engineer for the 2015 season. I was responsible for a team of 5 as well as the design and construction of the vehicle's composite fairing.
