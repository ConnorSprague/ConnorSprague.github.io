S+A Technologies Dermal Curette

S+A Technologies is a small company based out of Rolla Missouri focused on medical innovations ranging from medical apparatus to telemedicine.

One such medical device is the S+A Technologies Dermal Curette.

Much like the dichotomy between reusable shaving razors and plastic disposable razors, S+A Dermal Curettes aim to combine the professional precision feel of tradiotion Dermal Curettes with the affordability and low maintenance of disposable curettes.

S+A Dermal Curettes utilize an easy to exchange stainless steel blade mounted on a stainless steel handle. Eliminating the ecological impact of disposable blades while sustaining the precision of traditional systems.
